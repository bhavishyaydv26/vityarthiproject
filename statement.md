Problem statement:-
This project goal is to solve the problem of the people related to thier personal expense and how they can resolve this problem .The Tool is help you in record expense,view the total,understanding were they spend most in the spending category and you can easily fix any mistake in the record.

Scope of the Project:-
This project is a simple computer program only for managning your personal spending on your desktop.it stored data locally and limited to basic totals and categorgy report

Target users:-
1.target users is a simple tool to manage money and see thier spending on rent,food,bill and entertainment without needing complex features.

2.Students/Academics: Demonstrates a practical application of Python GUI development,data structure.

High-level features:-
1.Expense data management:-provides the fundamental capabilities to create(add),Read(view) and delete transactions
2.Instant total:-you can immediately see the total of all the money you are spending from your record
3.Spending Reports:-This will show a clear breakdown report showing exactly how much money you spent in each category.
